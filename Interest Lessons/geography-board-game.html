🧭 How to play
  • 🎲 Roll the dice and hop along the route from Vancouver, all the way around the world and back.
  • 🔵🟢🟠 Question tiles ask about your geography lessons. Answer right and you zoom 2 extra tiles ahead!
  • 🟡 Gold tiles are bonus world-explorer questions — worth 3 extra tiles.
  • 🟣 Chance tiles hide travel cards: tailwinds, container ships, monsoons, souvenirs…
  • 🛂 Passport control! Everyone must stop at the big stamp tiles. Answer a question about that continent to collect your stamp (you get the stamp either way!).
  • 🪿 Solo mode: Gale the Goose races you home. Don't worry if she wins — geese cheat with wings.
  • 🤝 Play Together: Grace and Warren take turns with the dice on the same computer. Each player answers questions at their own difficulty, collects their own stamps, and races to be first home. Gale referees!
  • 🏠 Reach Vancouver with all 6 stamps to complete your journey!
