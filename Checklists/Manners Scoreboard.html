How it works: Each kid starts the round with 10 green cards. A yellow card (−0.5) turns a green card half yellow; a red card (−1.0) takes a whole card — and two yellows = one red. Give a card by choosing its colour, typing the reason, and pressing Add — every card is logged with its date below. Stay at 5.0 or above (50%) to pass the week; miss it and there's no screen time the next week, with the bar raised to 60%.
