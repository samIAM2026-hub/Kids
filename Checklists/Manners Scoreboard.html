How it works: Each kid starts the round with 10 green cards. Every slip in manners is a yellow card (−0.5) — it turns a green card half yellow. A second yellow on that card makes it a whole red card (−1.0 lost). So two yellows = one red. Stay at 5.0 or above (that's 50%) to pass the week. Miss it and there's no screen time at all the next week — and next week's bar rises to 6.0 (60%). Flip on Penalty week for a kid who fell short.
